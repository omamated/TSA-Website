The Rosenberg/Richmond Community

Rosenberg

Rosenberg is a city located in Fort Bend County, Texas. It is part of the Houston metropolitan area and has a rich history dating back to the 19th century.

Richmond

Richmond is the county seat of Fort Bend County, Texas. It is situated along the Brazos River and is known for its historic downtown area and vibrant community events.

Community

Rosenberg and Richmond together form a connected community focused on growth, history, and local culture.
